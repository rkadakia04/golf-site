How to play

Three taps from challenge to chaos.

1

Challenge a friend

Open golf right in a message. Pick your ball, set the number of holes, and tap send — no app to download for them, it just shows up in the chat.

2

Take your shots

Pull back, aim, and swing through each colorful hole. Dodge the water and sand. Fewest strokes wins the match — so make every swing count.

3

Loser draws a card

Winner gets bragging rights. The loser? They pick a pack and draw a truth or a dare — sent straight back to the chat. Stakes make it fun.
