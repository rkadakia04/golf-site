golf, in your texts.

how to play

three taps from challenge to chaos.

1

pick your ball

start a match with a friend right in your chat and choose your golf ball. claim your icon, and it's game on.

2

take your shots

pull back, aim, and swing through each colorful hole. dodge the water and sand. fewest strokes wins the match, so make every swing count.

3

loser gets a card

the loser chooses a pack, and the winner deals them a card. stakes make it fun.
